# Hydro

This app allows one to look at (Ontario) Hydro One energy consumption in 
relation to local climate from Environment Canada. The user (you!) retrieve
'green button' energy consumption data from Hydro One to your local
device. Then click through the 'Hydro', 'Climate', and 'Insights' tabs to
see your consumption in relation to climate data.

The main purpose of this app was to learn more JavaScript. I wanted to learn
more of the [D3][] (data visualization) and [DataTables][] libraries, and to
explore the [mithril][] framework.

[D3]: https://d3js.org/
[DataTables]: https://datatables.net/
[mithril]: https://mithril.js.org/
